About Alice

Alice is a 14-year-old student passionate about science, literature, math, academic excellence, soft-academia aesthetics, and building a life with purpose.

She studies languages, writes essays, thinks deeply, dreams boldly, and works every day to become the first person in her family to attend Harvard.

This website is her laboratory — a place to publish ideas, essays, projects, and reflections as she shapes her future.
